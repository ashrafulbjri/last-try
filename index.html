Jute is a kind of fiber and it is collected from a plant. The plant grows from a height of six to ten feet. Generally, the jute tree has no branches. it has got barks and leaves which are bodily used. Jute grows well in hot and moist climates and needs marshy land. 80% of the worlds production of jute is grown in Bangladesh. Jute also grows in India, Japan, Thailand, and Indonesia. But Bangladesh grows the best jute. in fact, Bangladesh is the home of jute.
